About

I am Sajim Uddin, a dedicated and detail-oriented Test Automation Engineer based in Joypurhat, Bangladesh. I hold a Diploma in Computer Engineering and have developed a strong foundation in software testing and automation frameworks through real-world experience. I have worked as a Test Automation Engineer at Quality Up Services, where I was responsible for designing and executing automated tests for web and mobile applications. My work involved tools such as Selenium, Cypress, Postman, and Robot Framework. I collaborated with development and QA teams using Git, GitHub, Jira, and Azure Boards in agile environments. My programming proficiency includes Python, Java, and JavaScript. I’ve also utilized frameworks like Django, Behave, Cucumber, and Maven in various professional projects. I contributed to the automation of real-world platforms such as Daraz, Pickaboo, Google Task App, and a Django-based E-Commerce website. I specialize in Sanity Testing, Regression Testing, and Behavior-Driven Development (BDD). In addition to testing, I have hands-on experience in full-stack web development using Django, where I implemented features like CRUD operations, cart management, and payment gateway integration. My ability to write clean, structured code, combined with a keen eye for detail, allows me to identify issues early and ensure high software quality. I’m passionate about automation, continuous learning, and solving real-world problems through technology. Currently, I’m seeking a full-time or part-time opportunity where I can contribute to a dynamic QA team, grow professionally, and help deliver high-quality software products.
